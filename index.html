How We Help

A simple, supportive process designed to guide you through your care journey with clarity, comfort, and confidence. From your first conversation to ongoing support, we make every step easy & stress-free.

01

Assessment

We begin by listening. Our team takes the time to understand your needs, goals, preferences, and circumstances so we can provide care that truly fits your life.

02

Care Plan

Based on your assessment, we create a personalised care plan. This plan is flexible, transparent, and designed to support your independence and wellbeing.

03

Service Delivery

Our qualified & compassionate team delivers your care with respect, supporting you at home and in the community, exactly as per outlined in care plan.

04

Review

Your needs may change, and we regularly review your care to ensure it continues to meet your goals, making adjustments whenever needed.

Compassionate Care & Support Tailored for Every Need

At Southern Community Care, we provide personalised aged care, disability, and DVA services, alongside professional training for care providers. Our holistic approach ensures every individual receives the right support, guidance, and empowerment to live with independence, dignity, and confidence.

Comprehensive Aged Care Support

At Southern Community Care, we provide compassionate and personalised aged care services. From assessment and support planning to home care packages and the Commonwealth Home Support Programme (CHSP), our team ensures every client receives care tailored to their needs. We also manage case and care management, run engaging day programs and social groups, and conduct regular reviews to ensure your care evolves with you, promoting dignity, choice, and wellbeing.

Workplace Training and Care Provider Development

We are committed to strengthening the skills of care providers through workplace training programs. Our courses cover aged care, disability support, community consultation, cross-cultural communication, cultural responsiveness, diversity engagement, and diversity policy development. These programs equip providers with practical knowledge and sensitivity to deliver high-quality, inclusive care while promoting safer, more responsive, and culturally aware care environments.

Tailored NDIS Support Services

Navigating the NDIS can feel overwhelming, but our team makes it simple, helping participants achieve their goals and live fulfilling lives. We support every step from NDIS planning and pre-planning ensuring your plan aligns with your aspirations. Our trained staff deliver services through skilled support workers, and we assist with reviews and reporting, fostering independence, skill-building, and confidence.

Specialised DVA Support Services

We honour the service of veterans and their families by providing respectful and reliable DVA-funded support. Beginning with a needs assessment and care planning, we ensure each veteran receives personalised assistance. We also coordinate services, advocate on your behalf, and liaise with relevant organisations, helping veterans maintain wellbeing and enjoy the lifestyle they deserve while navigating DVA programs.
