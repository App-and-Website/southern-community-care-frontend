Resources and Links

At Southern Community Care, we believe in empowering you with the knowledge and tools to navigate your care journey with confidence. Below is a directory of essential Australian government and non-government services that can provide further information, support, and assistance.

Disclaimer: Southern Community Care is not responsible for the content of external websites. This list is provided for your convenience and information.

Aged Care

My Aged Care (Official Australian Government Gateway)

Website: https://www.myagedcare.gov.au

Phone: 1800 200 422

What you'll find: Information on eligibility, assessment, and finding approved aged care providers for services at home or in a facility.

Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission

Website: https://www.agedcarequality.gov.au

Phone: 1800 951 822

What you'll find: Information on your rights as an aged care consumer and how to make a complaint.

Carer Gateway (For Family and Friend Carers)

Website: https://www.carergateway.gov.au

Phone: 1800 422 737

What you'll find: Practical information and support services for carers, including counselling, crisis support, and planning.

Disability Support (NDIS)

NDIS – National Disability Insurance Scheme

Website: https://www.ndis.gov.au

Phone: 1800 800 110

What you'll find: Everything about the NDIS, including how to access the scheme, managing your plan, and finding providers.

NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission

Website: https://www.ndiscommission.gov.au

Phone: 1800 035 544

What you'll find: Information on NDIS provider standards, your rights as a participant, and how to raise a concern or complaint.

Veterans’ Affairs

Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA)

Website: https://www.dva.gov.au

Phone: 1800 838 372 (Open Arms) | 133 254 (General Enquiries)

What you'll find: Information on benefits, entitlements, and health services for current and former defence force members and their families.

Open Arms – Veterans and Families Counselling

Website: https://www.openarms.gov.au

Phone: 1800 011 046

What you'll find: Free and confidential, 24/7 crisis support and counselling for veterans and their families.

Crisis and Specialised Support

Ask Izzy: https://askizzy.org.au

Salvation Army: https://www.salvationarmy.org.au

Mission Australia: https://www.missionaustralia.com.au

Mental Health Support

Lifeline: 13 11 14 | https://www.lifeline.org.au

Beyond Blue: 1300 22 4636 | https://www.beyondblue.org.au

Headspace: 1800 650 890 | https://headspace.org.au

Family and Domestic Violence (FDV) Support

1800RESPECT: 1800 737 732 | https://www.1800respect.org.au

Full Stop Australia: 1800 385 578 | https://fullstop.org.au

Legal Aid

National Legal Aid

Website: https://www.nationallegalaid.org

What you'll find: Links to your state or territory's Legal Aid commission for free legal advice and representation.
