Our Mission: To Empower Every Journey with Dignity and Respect

At Southern Community Care, our mission is deeply personal. We are dedicated to empowering individuals to live their lives with independence, dignity, and choice. We believe that everyone, regardless of age or ability, deserves to write their own story and feel supported in their own home and community.

We walk alongside you, whether you are navigating the complexities of aging, living with a disability, or have served our country providing the compassionate support and expert guidance that turns challenges into opportunities for growth and connection.

Our Values: The Heart of Our Care

Our values are not just words on a page; they are the promises we keep every day. They guide our actions, shape our culture, and define the quality of our relationships with you.

Compassion

We listen with our hearts and seek to understand your unique story, needs, and aspirations. Our care is delivered with genuine kindness and empathy, creating a foundation of trust and comfort.

Integrity

We are committed to being open, honest, and ethical in everything we do. We build trust through transparency, reliability, and by always acting in your best interests.

Empowerment

We focus on your strengths and capabilities. Our role is to provide you with the resources, support, and confidence to make your own choices, set your own goals, and maintain control over your life.

Respect

We honour your life experiences, your decisions, and your right to privacy. We treat every person as a unique individual, valuing their voice and celebrating their identity.

Collaboration

We achieve the best outcomes by working together with you, your family, and your wider support network. By fostering strong partnerships, we create a circle of care greater than the sum of its parts.

Governance and Compliance: Your Safety and Quality Are Our Priority

At Southern Community Care, we believe that exceptional care is built on a foundation of trust, safety, and rigorous standards. Our commitment to excellence is demonstrated through our formal accreditation and unwavering adherence to the highest levels of governance and compliance.

Our Accredited Commitment to Excellence

NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission

We are a registered NDIS provider, meeting all the strict requirements of the NDIS Practice Standards. This ensures our services are effective, person-centred, and uphold participants’ rights to choice and control.

Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission

We are accredited against the Aged Care Quality Standards. This independent endorsement means our aged care services are safe, respectful, and focused on your health, wellbeing, and quality of life.

Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA)

We are an approved provider for DVA services, adhering to the specific standards and requirements set for the care and support of our valued veterans and their families.

Our Framework for Safe, High-Quality Care

  • Client Safety First: Robust risk management and safeguarding policies protect you from harm and ensure a safe environment for clients and staff.
  • Rigorous Staff Screening and Training: Team members are carefully selected, qualified, and supported through ongoing compliance and best-practice training.
  • Your Rights Are Paramount: We uphold your rights to privacy, dignity, independence, and informed decision-making.
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular audits, monitoring, and client feedback help us grow and improve continuously.
  • Transparent and Accountable: We operate with integrity and transparency in all financial and reporting practices.

You can be assured that when you choose Southern Community Care, you are partnering with an organisation committed not just to meeting standards, but to exceeding them every day, for you.
