Make a Referral

Referring someone to Southern Community Care is simple and confidential. Whether you are a health professional, a family member, or making a self-referral, our team will guide you through the next steps with care and respect.

1

Complete the referral form with the relevant details.

2

Our team reviews the information and contacts you.

3

We arrange a discussion or assessment for next steps.

Southern Community Care provides compassionate aged care, NDIS, and DVA support services in Sydney. Our personalised care helps you or your loved ones live safely, independently, and with dignity in the comfort of your home.
